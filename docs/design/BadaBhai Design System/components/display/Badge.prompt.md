Status pill for verification, job state, and counts.

```jsx
<Badge tone="success" icon="seal-check">Verified</Badge>
<Badge tone="warning" upper>Paused</Badge>
<Badge tone="brand" variant="solid">2 left</Badge>
```

- The Verified badge = `tone="success" icon="seal-check"`.
- `upper` for short status words (PAUSED, CLOSED); leave off for counts.
