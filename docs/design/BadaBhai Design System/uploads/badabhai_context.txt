1 · Product in one page
BadaBhai turns blue/grey-collar workers in India into live, profiled, contactable candidates — profiled by a conversational AI ("bada bhai," the helpful big brother who never tests, only helps) in Hinglish/regional language, chat + voice — and sells reach and access to anyone who pays: vacancy-banded job postings, boosts, and contact unlocks. Plus, for every worker: a free, perfect, AI-generated resume and a trade interview kit — the marketing front door. Two client surfaces: a worker mobile app and a Company/Agency web app. Not a staffing firm, not an employer-of-record, not a static job board. Money in → reach + data + access out.

The moat: chat-built profiles (workers who could never write a resume) + live intent + the compounding behavioral event stream + the indefinitely-retained voice/transcript corpus (model-training consent from day one) + the resume artifact base.

Wedge: Phase 1–2 industrial — 15 trades built (the 7 launch CNC/VMC roles + adjacent manufacturing trades, RVM-ratified 2026-06-17). Phase 3 hospitality (content pending product sign-off), Phase 4 gig. Primary strategic threat: Vahan.ai (manufacturing expansion underway); the CNC/VMC AI-placement niche is genuinely unoccupied but time-limited. Strongest moats: RVM institutional supply + CNC/VMC domain depth.

Positioning: Naukri-Resdex-for-blue-collar with live intent + a posting product + the resume hook — non-competitive with staffing firms (customers, not rivals; no payroll/EOR) — opposite of Vahan's supply model while copying its Mitra-Leader referral mechanic. The long-horizon vision is a workforce-intelligence data layer (DaaS) — every profiled worker, on-wedge or not, is part of the asset.

3A · THE EIGHT CEO-LOCKED DECISIONS (2026-06-19) [LOCKED]
Company job-posting price — Land-grab + guardrail. Posting stays free through launch (small/single-vacancy bands free forever; bigger bands free during the 15–30-day intro, then cheap). Verification-gated so ghost jobs don't waste worker swipes or erode trust. Monetize hard on unlocks, not posting. Revisit posting fees only once liquidity is proven. (Drives a "is this job real" check — distinct from payer-identity verification, which we still don't do.)
Agency job-posting price — same as company. No agency-specific posting pricing at launch. Agency value is already captured two ways (their 25% supply rev-share and their unlock volume). Net-contribution models parked.
Profile-unlock price — ₹40 flat. Packs of 50 / 200 / 1,000 with a real discount on the 1,000-pack (steers high-volume accounts to bulk → recurring revenue). Same price for agencies and companies. Logic: with the 5-unlocks/worker/week cap a worker earns max ~₹200/week, so revenue comes from worker volume → price for adoption, not extraction. Tune within 60 days on real buying.
WhatsApp — 5 nudges, all opt-in, all pointing back into the app. The four (invite · job alert · contact notification · come-back nudge) plus "your resume is ready — download it" (closes the completion loop, pulls workers back). Referral nudge held until the referral mechanic is live (parked). Watch opt-out rates — they kill deliverability. The conversation itself stays in-app (the moat).
North star — weekly PAID unlocks. Exclude seeded free credits so the number isn't flattered. Hero health metric = repeat-unlock rate (do payers come back? — the retention/NRR proof for the seed round). Dashboard to compute both still needs building (BUG-2).
Multi-trade visitors — welcome all + free resume + waitlist, seen through the DaaS lens (every profiled worker is the data asset, ~₹4 CAC is trivial, it pre-fills trades before launch). Guardrail: off-wedge handling is 100% self-serve (resume + waitlist + alert, zero human ops) so it never drains the lean team; waitlist messaging stays honest (no implied imminent launch).
Resume design — one excellent template per trade-family, subtly BadaBhai-branded + WhatsApp-share-optimized (every forwarded resume = free acquisition). The edit/enrich action quietly deepens the profile (resume edit = more profiling — the Trojan horse). Worker controls only safe fields (name spelling, photo on/off, phone shown/hidden, language). Multiple styles held for later, demand-driven.
Reach weights — confirmed 35/20/15/15/10/5 as v1 cold-start defaults. Engineering reconciles the diverged code (ADR-0006) to these. No city-tuning at launch — ship flat, tune with data once the Learn layer has unlock/apply behavior. No gender/age/caste/religion — unambiguous yes (core to fairness positioning and real ethical/regulatory protection).

Caps [PROPOSED, INERT]: 5 unlocks/worker/7d (15/30d) · 50/day/account · per-payer capacity not enforced (TD43). Exact rupee numbers for posting bands still come from the CBA (≤ Jul 2); the unlock price (₹40) is now fixed.
